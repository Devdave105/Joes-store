Our Story

Elegance is not a
luxury — it's a right.

Joe's Store was born from a simple belief: every woman in Ghana deserves to dress beautifully without compromising her budget or comfort. Founded in Accra, we've been dressing confident, modern women since 2018 — one palazzo pant at a time.

We source only the finest fabrics, design with the African woman's figure in mind, and deliver directly to your door with care and speed. From Madina to Kumasi, from Ho to Tamale — we dress Ghana's women with pride.

FAQ

Common Questions

We deliver nationwide within 2–3 business days. For orders within Accra, same-day or next-day delivery is often available. You'll receive a tracking update via WhatsApp once your order ships.

Each product page includes a detailed size guide. We offer sizes S, M, L, and XL. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a more comfortable fit. You can also message us on WhatsApp and we'll help you choose the right size.

You can add items to your cart and checkout via WhatsApp for personalised service. Alternatively, click the "Order on WhatsApp" button on any product page to send us a direct message with your selection.

We accept Mobile Money (MTN MoMo, Vodafone Cash, AirtelTigo Money), bank transfers, and cash on delivery within Accra. Payment details are confirmed when you place your order via WhatsApp.

Yes! We offer returns or exchanges within 7 days of delivery for unworn, unwashed items with original tags. Contact us on WhatsApp to initiate a return. Your satisfaction is our priority.
